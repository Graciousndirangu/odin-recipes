The best Bacon recipe we have

This recipe brought home the bacon (and the trophy).

Ingredients

  • Pie Crust
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Mix bacon, chopped onion, and both cheeses in a medium bowl.
  3. Mix eggs and half and half in a bowl until blended;
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes.
