The Best Chicken Recipe of 2023 Is "Perfectly Fried"

Oven-Fried Chicken

I love fried chicken, but it takes so long and is such a big mess. I came up with
this oven-fried version, and have been making it ever since! Everyone I have
given the recipe to has loved it as well.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Seasoning
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place flour in a shallow plate or bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Dredge chicken piece by piece in the flour, then the egg, then the bread crumbs, until all pieces are coated.
  4. Pour oil into a large baking dish; add the breaded chicken thighs and sprinkle with paprika to taste.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then turn pieces over and bake for another 30 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and Enjoy
